Â¿Chico-UI?

Is the open sourced MercadoLibre's visual framework. The main goal is to improve and accelerate the development proccess. Is builded on top of jQuery, this toolbox brings a couple of known visual patterns for the web and takes advantages over the new HTML5 and CSS3 features.

Chico-UI team is available to any feedback or suggestions. We want to share this tool with all designers and developers to make the web beautiful.
